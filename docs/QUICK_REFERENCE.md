## 🚀 Quick Start

### For Individual Users

1. **Login**: Use your @presidio.com email
   ```
   Example: bcherukuri@presidio.com
   ```

2. **Navigate**: Dashboard loads automatically at `/individual`

3. **Interact with Quick Actions**:
   - 📥 Export Report (download your data)
   - 💡 View Performance Tips (get suggestions)
   - 🎯 Set Goals (coming soon)

4. **Hover Over Metrics**: Position mouse over stat cards to see detailed information

5. **Select Time Period**: Click one of the buttons
   - 📅 Weekly (8 weeks)
   - 📅 Monthly (6 months)
   - 📅 Quarterly (4 quarters)
   - 📅 Yearly (3 years)

6. **View Your Metrics**:
   - 🎯 Current utilization percentage
   - ⏱️ Hours breakdown (project vs. fringe)
   - 📈 Performance trends
   - 🏆 Progress milestones & achievements

## 💡 Pro Tips

### Maximize Your Dashboard

1. **Use Quick Actions**: Export your data regularly for personal records
2. **Read Performance Tips**: Click "View Tips" for personalized suggestions
3. **Hover for Details**: Position mouse over metric cards for explanations
4. **Track Achievements**: Work toward unlocking all 6 badges
5. **Check Month-on-Month**: See detailed breakdowns of your progress
6. **Review Weekly**: Check your dashboard every Monday morning
7. **Set a Routine**: Consistent 90%+ utilization is the goal
8. **Watch Trends**: Look for patterns in your performance
9. **Use Comparisons**: See your period-over-period growth
10. **Support Mentees**: If you're a mentor, check in regularly

### Using Interactive Features

**Interactive Tooltips**:
- Hover over the 4 metric cards in the hero section
- See detailed breakdowns and explanations
- No clicking required - just hover!

**Quick Actions**:
- **Export**: Downloads a JSON file with your current data
- **Tips**: Shows/hides personalized improvement suggestions
- **Goals**: Coming soon - set custom performance targets

**Achievements**:
- Each badge shows your progress (percentage complete)
- Hover over badges to see requirements
- Completed badges are fully colored
- In-progress badges show a progress ring

### Understanding Your Data

- **Target Hours**: Your expected billable hours
- **Project Hours**: Actual hours logged to projects
- **Utilization**: Project Hours ÷ Target Hours × 100
- **Fringe**: Non-billable but necessary activities
- **W/Presales**: Utilization including presales work
- **Efficiency Score**: Overall performance metric (in comparisons)

### What to Look For

✅ **Good Signs**:
- Consistent 90%+ utilization
- Upward trend in charts
- Meeting milestones on time
- Positive period comparisons
- Unlocking achievement badges
- Green trend indicators (↑)

⚠️ **Warning Signs**:
- Declining trend over multiple periods
- Consistently <70% utilization
- Large gaps between target and actual hours
- Red indicators on multiple metrics
- Negative month-on-month changes
- Down trend indicators (↓)

## 🆘 Troubleshooting

### No Data Showing?
1. Verify you're logged in
2. Contact your manager to upload data
3. Check that your name matches exactly in the system

### Charts Not Loading?
1. Refresh the page
2. Clear browser cache
3. Try a different browser
4. Check for JavaScript errors (F12 → Console)

### Performance Slow?
1. Close unused browser tabs
2. Clear browser cache
3. Use latest browser version
4. Contact IT if issue persists
